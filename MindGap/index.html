The Levels of AI Maturity

L0

Lookup

AI as search engine. Query-response interactions for information retrieval. No generation, no workflow integration.

L1

Autocomplete

Human-led, AI-assisted. You write, the model suggests. Manual prompting, low autonomy, reactive support on demand.

L2

Bottleneck

AI generates, humans verify. High-volume output with mandatory review of every result. Scale limited by approval capacity and trust bandwidth.

L3

Policy

Human governs, agent executes. Rules-based delegation where humans set constraints and AI operates within them.

L4

Vision

Humans define success, agents deliver. Outcome-oriented delegation with human-set metrics and autonomous AI execution.

The Technology Is Ready. Are You?

Today’s AI operates at Level 4. Autonomous execution against defined outcomes. Meanwhile, most organisations remain structured for Levels 0 through 2 — manual prompting, human review of every output, approval bottlenecks that constrain scale.

The gap between capability and adoption is not technical. It is organisational. MindGap measures that distance.

The Problem
